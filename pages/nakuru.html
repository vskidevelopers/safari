Malikale Kenya Presents:

4-Day Masai Mara and Lake Nakuru

Day 1: Nairobi-Masai Mara

Meal plan: Breakfast,lunch,dinner
0700hrs:Pick up from Nairobi hotel / Nairobi Airport Depart for the lake Nakuru national park. An easy scenic sightseeing drive via the great rift valley escarpment with views of awesome scenery There shall be stops to marvel at the dramatic fall of the great rift valley escarpment. We shall stop at the Freshwater Lake Naivasha for an exciting boat ride to view hippos, exotic birds and small game. This shall be followed by guided walking safari at the Crescent Island Later proceed to the Lake Nakuru arriving during the late morning. Read More.. The Lake Nakuru national park is home to black and white rhino, lions, warthogs, giraffes, zebra and other small game. It also hosts large number of bird species including Pelicans and sometimes a sizable Flamingo population. Afternoon shall be spent on bird and game watching drives around the Lake. Overnight at your lake Nakuru national park safari lodge/ tented camp
Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve
Accommodation: Budget tented camp located just outside Masai Mara NR
Read Less..

Day 2: Masai Mara Extensive drive

Meals & Drinks: All meals included Drinks not included
Full day of game drive, breakfast will be served as early as 0630am, then with picnic lunch since the whole day will be exploring the Masai Mara famous 7th wonders of the World. This game reserve is Kenya's finest wildlife sanctuary. Including migrants, well over 450 species of animals have been recorded here. Mara plains are filled with migrating wildebeest and zebra, there is also resident wildlife year round. The Mara's gentle fertile climate bounded by The Rift, offers perhaps the richest stocked reserve set within some 1672 sq km -

Read More an unlimited spectacle of wildlife. Located 268 km west of Nairobi, the emphasis here is on game. Herds of immense Elephants, browse among Lion, Buffalo, Thompson Gazelle, Zebra and Topi. You may occasionally sight black Rhino. In the Mara River Hippos play and Cross bask on the banks. The annual migration of a million Wildebeest follows the rains north. After exploring the park return to the camp or Lodge for dinner and overnight. Masai Mara National Reserve
Accommodation: Budget tented camp located just outside Masai Mara NR

Day 3: Masai Mara - Nakuru

Meals & Drinks: All meals included Drinks not included .
You will have a wake up call as early as 0600am, you will have hot African Coffee or Tea and then proceed for pre - morning game drive as from 06:30hrs for the next 3 hours until 0930hrs, then our driver will drive you back to the camp where you will be served with full breakfast as you check out the camp to proceed to Lake Nakuru, if willing on this day you can as well visit the Masai Village for one hour and learn more of the Masai Culture,

Read More lunch will be in Narok town or ( Mai Mahiu) hot water after lunch proceed to Lake Nakuru arriving in the evening and head straight to a Budget hotel for evening relaxation.
Main Destination: Lake Nakuru National Park
Accommodation: Budget hotel located outside Lake Nakuru NP

Day 4: Nakuru - Nairobi

Meals & Drinks: Breakfast & lunch (Dinner not included) Drinking water (Other drinks not included)
Breakfast will be served at around 0700am there after proceed for the game drive in Lake Nakuru National Park, you will have a chance to visit a unique sanctuary catering for a sizable number of the endangered White and Black rhinos as well as Rothschild giraffe. Water bucks, predators such as the lion and leopard are common at the park. Also to be seen is a myriad different variety of bird life in and around the lake.

Read More Flamingo's are also plenty at the lake. Leave for Nairobi late afternoon arriving in Nairobi in the afternoon with a drop off to the hotel of your choice or back to the airport to catch up with your flight for the next destination.
Destination: Nairobi (City)
Accommodation: No accommodation (End of tour)
