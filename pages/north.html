Hello, welcome to Mali kale Kenya!

7-Day Northern & Southern Kenya Savannah Private Safari

Day 1 Nairobi to Samburu National Reserve

Meals & Drinks: Lunch & dinner (Breakfast not included)
Drinking water (Other drinks not included)
7.30 am - Pick up from the hotel within Nairobi or the airport. Drive to Samburu National reserve which takes an approximate of 6 hrs. On arrival have lunch and check-in at the lodge and relax. After lunch and relaxation proceed for an afternoon game drive until the African Sunset. Return back towards the lodge for dinner and overnight stay.
Main Destination: Samburu National Reserve
Accommodation: Mid-range lodge bordering Samburu NR without fences

Day 2 Samburu national reserve game drives

Meals & Drinks: Lunch & dinner (Breakfast not included)
Drinking water (Other drinks not included)
Early breakfast at the lodge After breakfast head out for a morning game drive and enjoy views of variety of wildlife such as the famous Grevy zebra, lions, leopards, and elephants. Return towards the lodge for hot lunch and relaxation. Thereafter proceed for the second game drive until the African sunset. Return towards the lodge for dinner and overnight stay.
Destination: Samburu National Reserve
Accommodation: Mid-range lodge bordering Samburu NR without fences

Day 3 Samburu national reserve to Lake Nakuru National park

Meals & Drinks: All meals included
Drinking water (Other drinks not included)
Early breakfast at the lodge After breakfast check out and drive towards the Rift Valley region to Lake Nakuru. On arrival check-in and have lunch. After lunch and relaxation proceed for an afternoon game drive till late evening. Return towards the lodge for dinner and overnight stay.
Main Destination: Lake Nakuru National Park
Accommodation: Mid-range lodge located just outside Lake Nakuru NP

Day 4 Lake Nakuru National Park to Lake Naivasha

Meals & Drinks: All meals included
Drinking water (Other drinks not included)
Early breakfast at the hotel. After breakfast drive towards Lake Naivasha and directly to Hell's gate National Park. Enjoy short excursions of hiking and game drives. Afterward, proceed to the hotel and check-in and have lunch. After lunch and relaxation drive to Lake Naivasha and enjoy afternoon boat rides at the Lakeshores until the African Sunset with great views of hippos. Return to the hotel and have dinner and overnight stay.
Main Destination: Lake Naivasha (Lake)
Accommodation: Budget hotel located in or near Lake Naivasha

Day 5 Lake Naivasha to Masai Mara National Reserve

Meals & Drinks: All meals included
Drinking water (Other drinks not included)
Early breakfast at the lodge. Check out at the lodge and drive to Masai Mara which takes an approximate of 6 hrs drive. On arrival check-in and have lunch. After lunch and relaxation proceed for an afternoon game drive until the African sunset. Return towards lodge or camp for dinner and overnight stay.
Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve
Accomodation : Mid-range tented camp located just outside Masai Mara NR

Day 6 Masai Mara game drives

Meals & Drinks: All meals included
Drinking water (Other drinks not included)
Early breakfast at the camp or lodge. After breakfast proceed for a full day game drive with packed lunch. While on the full day game drives enjoy views of great amounts of wildlife such as the African Big five which are Lion, Leopard, Elephant, Buffalo, and Rhino. Most time is spent while trying to search for these animals during game drives. Return towards lodge or camp for dinner and overnight stay.
Main Destination: Masai Mara National Reserve
Accommodation: Mid-range tented camp located just outside Masai Mara NR

Day 7 Masai mara to nairobi

Meals & Drinks: Breakfast (Lunch & dinner not included)
Drinking water (Other drinks not included)
Early sunrise game drives in the reserve. Return back to camp or Lodge for breakfast. After breakfast check out and drive towards Nairobi which takes an approximate of 5 to 6 hrs drive.
Main Destination: No major destination visited
Accommodation: No accommodation (End of tour)
